Our Story

Founded in 1978, Jayantilal Narotamdas & Co. emerged from a profound appreciation for rich textiles and impeccable design. What began as a dedicated family pursuit has matured over nearly five decades into a premier legacy brand, serving couturiers, luxury interior creators, and master tailors across generations.

Our specialty resides in selecting the absolute highest tier of velvets and artisanal fabrics. From brilliant depth of color to magnificent, heavy-draped lusters, every single meter we supply carries the meticulous stamp of family oversight. We do not just source material; we secure the very foundational textures of architectural and fashion masterpieces.

Uncompromised Selection

Every production run undergoes strict, hands-on quality testing for color accuracy, weight density, and structural durability.

Family Devotion

As a multigenerational business, our word is our bond. We build real relationships with our design partners and clients.
